The Bridge Between Analysis and Empathy

Life can be messy. The challenges we face each day can be overwhelming, and navigating them alone can seem impossible. That's where I come in. Peers in my field have described me as 'the balance of masculine and feminine energy'; this I use to assist in gaining clarity and purpose in life for those who seek it.



My path to becoming a mental health professional wasn't traditional. Before becoming a counsellor, I spent two decades navigating the corporate world, analysing data and leading teams. This experience honed my analytical skills, igniting a passion for understanding the human side of things once becoming a people leader â€“ the emotions, experiences, and relationships that make us who we are. My additional background working in Correctional Services highlighted the complex challenges individuals face. That's why I bridge the gap between analysis and empathy. I can help you identify your patterns and thought processes while creating a space for understanding and compassion.



In my sessions, authenticity is the key. There's no judgment here, and no topic is taboo. I believe true healing comes from a place of complete openness and honesty. Together, we can create a safe environment where you feel empowered to be authentic, your true sense of self.

My lived experiences as a husband, father, and someone who has navigated relationship challenges, complex family dynamics, addiction, anxiety, depression, and neurodiversity inform my approach. I understand these struggles firsthand, walking alongside you with empathy and understanding.

Confidentiality is paramount. Everything you share in our sessions is kept strictly confidential.

I believe in using evidence-based practices to guide your journey, ensuring you receive the most effective support. There are no gimmicks, hidden agendas or lectures here - the science behind human psychology is used to provide complete autonomy of your circumstances.

If you seek a down-to-earth mental health professional who will take the time to understand, who prioritises authenticity and creates a safe space for healing, then I might be a good fit for you. While working with me, "it is your world and I am simply living in it".
