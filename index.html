Counselling Services


Welcome to my official website. I offer confidential counselling services to help individuals navigate life's challenges and achieve emotional well-being. I pride myself on creating a safe space to foster authenticity.



Bridging the Gap Between Analysis and Empathy

For over 15 years, I navigated the world of data analysis and leadership roles. In correctional services, I witnessed firsthand the challenges individuals face. This experience, coupled with my Bachelor's degree in Computing, ignited a passion for understanding human behavior and the desire to create positive change.

My journey then led me to pursue a Master's degree in Counselling. Here, I honed my skills in active listening, empathy, and evidence-based therapeutic techniques. Today, I bring this unique blend of experience and knowledge to my counselling practice.

By combining data analysis with a deep understanding of human emotions, I help clients gain valuable insights into their thoughts, feelings, and patterns. This empowers them to make informed choices and navigate life's complexities with greater clarity and confidence.


What sets my approach apart?

  • A data-driven perspective: I believe in using evidence-based practices to guide your journey, ensuring you receive the most effective support.
  • Real-world experience: My background in leadership, correctional services, relationships and fatherhood allows me to understand the challenges you face and offer practical strategies for overcoming them.
  • Compassionate communication: I create a safe and supportive space for open communication, where you can explore your thoughts and emotions without judgment.

My goal is to walk alongside you on your own authentic path to personal growth. Let's work together to uncover your strengths, build resilience, and achieve the well-being you deserve.
